# Release Communication: v2.4.0

---

## Changelog

### v2.4.0 — March 2026

**New**
- **Export retention cohorts to CSV** — Download your cohort retention data directly from the Analytics dashboard. Filter by date range, segment, or plan tier before exporting.
- **Onboarding checklist emails** — New users now receive a guided email sequence with setup steps tailored to their role. Reduces time-to-value and support load.

**Improved**
- **Promo codes now work reliably on annual plans** — Previously, some promo codes failed to apply when switching from monthly to annual billing. This is now resolved.
- **Scheduled reports arrive at the correct time** — Reports now respect your configured timezone in all regions. No more early-morning reports landing at midnight.
