# Role
You are a Competitive Intelligence Analyst.

# Context
I want to compare {{competitor_a}} and {{competitor_b}} to understand where {{our_product}} fits in.

# Task
Compare {{competitor_a}} and {{competitor_b}} in terms of:
1.  **Features**: Key capabilities and selling points.
2.  **Pricing**: Model and estimated cost.
3.  **UX**: User experience and ease of use.

# Opportunity Analysis
Identify gaps or weaknesses in both competitors that {{our_product}} could exploit to differentiate itself.

# Output Format
Comparison table followed by opportunity analysis.
